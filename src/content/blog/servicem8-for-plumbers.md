## What Australian Plumbers Actually Need From Software

Generic business software doesn't map to plumbing operations. What plumbers need is specific:

**Compliance certificate management.** Plumbing work in Australia requires compliance certificates under state legislation — Certificates of Compliance in Victoria, Certificates of Compliance (Plumbing Work) in NSW, and equivalent instruments across every state and territory. ## Compliance Documentation: The Foundation

Plumbing compliance requirements vary by state, but the common thread is this: work must be documented, certificates must be issued, and records must be maintained. The penalties for non-compliance are significant — licence suspension, fines, and in serious cases, personal liability.

### Custom Digital Compliance Forms

ServiceM8's form builder lets you create compliance checklists and certificate templates that field staff complete on-site, before marking a job done. You configure what's required:

- Pre-work isolation and safety checks
- Backflow prevention verification
- Pressure testing results
- Water efficiency compliance (WELS ratings, AS/NZS 6400)
- Hot water system temperature checks (Legionella prevention, per AS/NZS 3498)
- Gas connection certification where applicable

Every field must be completed before the job can be marked complete. No more chasing techs for paperwork a week later.

### State-by-State Certificate Templates

Victoria requires plumbing compliance certificates under the Plumbing and Draining Act 1988 as enforced by the VBA (Victorian Building Authority). NSW operates under the Plumbing and Drainage Act 2011 via NSW Fair Trading. Queensland has its own framework under the Plumbing and Drainage Act 2018.

In ServiceM8, you build a separate form template for each jurisdiction. When a job is created in Victoria, the tech gets the VBA-formatted compliance template. In Queensland, they get the Queensland version. The right documentation, every time, without manual intervention.

For plumbing businesses operating across state lines — increasingly common for commercial and multi-site clients — this is operationally significant.

### Photo Documentation

When a plumber photographs a completed installation, a pressure gauge reading, or a problematic pipe through the ServiceM8 app, that photo is automatically attached to the job record with a timestamp and GPS coordinates.

For compliance audits, insurance claims, or customer disputes, you can pull up every photo from every job in seconds. The days of "I'm sure the photo is somewhere on Jake's phone" are over.

## Scheduling and Dispatch for Plumbing Teams

Emergency callouts are part of plumbing life. A burst pipe, a blocked drain, a hot water system failure — customers call expecting fast response. If you don't have visibility across your team, responding efficiently is guesswork.

ServiceM8's dispatch board gives you a live view of all jobs and all field plumbers, with GPS tracking showing exactly where everyone is. When the emergency call comes in:

1. You see which plumber is closest to the job location
2. You check what they're currently doing and when they'll be free
3. You assign the callout in seconds and they receive it immediately on their phone
4. The client gets an automated SMS with confirmation and an ETA

No phone tag. No "I'll call you back when I've checked the schedule." Immediate, professional response — which is exactly what wins service call jobs over competitors.
