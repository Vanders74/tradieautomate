## What Is ServiceM8?

ServiceM8 is a cloud-based job management platform built specifically for trade and field service businesses. Founded in Australia, it's designed around the way tradies actually work — mobile-first, compliance-aware, and tightly integrated with the tools Australian businesses rely on, like Xero and MYOB.

At its core, ServiceM8 handles:

- **Job booking and scheduling** — drag-and-drop dispatch board, GPS tracking, arrival ETAs
- **Quoting and invoicing** — build quotes on-site, convert to invoice instantly, accept card payments
- **Client communications** — automated SMS reminders, job confirmations, and follow-ups
- **Compliance documentation** — digital forms, photo capture, certificate generation
- **Staff management** — time tracking, job notes, and task checklists per job

It runs as an iOS and Android app for field staff, with a web-based admin dashboard for office management.

## Limitations

**No native project management for large jobs.** ServiceM8 is optimised for single-visit or multi-stage jobs, but it's not designed for complex, multi-month construction projects with cost tracking across dozens of cost codes. If you're doing large commercial solar farms, you may need to supplement with project management tooling.

**iOS-first experience.** While Android works, the iOS app has historically received features and polish first. Android users report a minor but noticeable gap in experience.

**Add-on costs add up.** The base plan is affordable, but enabling advanced features like route optimisation, job costing, or certain integrations pushes the monthly cost higher than the headline price suggests. Calculate total cost carefully before committing.

**Limited custom reporting.** Out-of-the-box reporting is functional but basic. Businesses that need deep operational analytics will find the built-in reports limiting and may need to export data to a separate BI tool.

**Not ideal for enterprise scale.** ServiceM8 is optimised for businesses with 1–50 field staff. Beyond that, platforms like simPRO with dedicated project management modules may be more appropriate.

## Getting the Most from ServiceM8: Tips from Power Users

**Build your job templates first.** Before going live, invest time building job templates for your 5 most common job types. Include required forms, checklists, and photo steps. This one-time setup makes every future job faster to administer.

**Use the dispatch board daily.** The visual dispatch board is most powerful when it's used as the single source of truth for the workday — not just as a reference. Train your office staff to manage the entire day through it.

**Set up Xero sync from day one.** Don't wait until you've been using ServiceM8 for a few months to connect Xero. The sooner you have clean financial records flowing through, the more useful your historical data becomes.

**Enable automated messaging.** The default templates for job reminders, arrival notifications, and invoice follow-ups are good starting points. Customise the language to match your brand voice — but enable them immediately rather than waiting to perfect them.
